Overview

As someone who is deeply interested in renewable energy and the optimization of energy systems, I am excited to share my knowledge and educate others through this blog. I have a strong passion for these topics and have had the opportunity to work on a variety of projects in this field. If you have any suggestions for topics to discuss, please don't hesitate to reach out. You can find more information about me on the About, as well as a summary of my research and professional experiences on the Experiences page.
