    Hiking and Travelling


    Hiking is one of my all-time favorite hobbies and I have been fortunate enough to experience some incredible hiking routes in Kathmandu valley. From Chitlang to Markhu, Fulchowki to Kalinchowk, Taarevir, and Chisapani, I have had the opportunity to explore the natural beauty of Nepal with my friends. The trails in Kathmandu valley offer breathtaking views of the mountains, forests, and villages, and the sense of accomplishment upon reaching the summit is unparalleled. The friendly locals and delicious food also add to the charm of hiking in Nepal. I highly recommend visiting these locations and experiencing the joy of hiking in this beautiful country. The natural beauty of Nepal is something that needs to be seen firsthand and is worth a visit in your free time. Trust me, you won't be disappointed.
